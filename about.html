About Me


My story

I am a Materials Science Engineer currently living in Kolkata, India. My aim in my research is to pursue the study of Computational Materials Science, with specializations in Density Functional Theory (DFT) and MD Simulations to further develop Novel Materials for Energy Storage whilst aiming for a clean, green, Sustainable-Energy driven economy in the near future.


I am also a member of the Royal Aeronautical Society, London. My interests range from photography to cycling. I am also interested in hiking, food, and aviation.
