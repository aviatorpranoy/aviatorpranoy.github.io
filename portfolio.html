Projects

First Principles Study of Hydrogen Storage in Transition-Metal doped Carbon Nanostructures
HP & SRPD, BARC
This ongoing project is being conducted under the supervision of Dr. B. Chakraborty, Scientist F of the High Pressure & Synchrotron Radiation Physics Division, BARC. The determination of Hydrogen Storage Capabilities of various new materials in accordance with the DOE's standards is being analyzed using BARC's supercomputing facility.

Determination of Water Contamination Content Profile in a Steel Plant Belt(Durgapur)
Physics Department, NIT Durgapur
 This ongoing project is being conducted under the supervision of Prof. Hirok Chaudhari of the Physics Department, NIT Durgapur. The determination of the content profile and their effect in our daily lives, besides a few other detrimental physical factors are measured.

Identification of Post Investment Casting Defects, HAL(F&F)
Development Department, F&F Division, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
 The project was completed in the Development Department (F&F), of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Bangalore, aims at the identification of various defects that can be detected post-casting only like microshrinkage, blowholes, etc., their analysis and finally their mitigation.

Preventive Measures for Quality Control using Lean Management, HAL(F&F)
Quality Control Department, F&F Division, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
 The project was completed in the Quality Control Department (F&F), of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Bangalore, aims at designing of Fish-Bone (Fishikawa) Diagrams to mitigate the errors caused during various sectors of the casting process used to manufacture various aircraft parts, with materials such as Mn-alloys, Al-alloys, etc..
